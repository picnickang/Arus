STEP 3 — SCHEDULING ENGINE (PHASE 1 GREEDY)
====================================================
Implement a scheduling service that generates a proposed schedule for a date range (default next 14 days):

Inputs:
- list of TaskDTOs
- capacity model (simple config for now):
  - daily_total_crew_hours
  - optional skill-specific caps
- optional vessel availability windows (can default to always available)
- lead times:
  - approval_lead_time_days
  - parts_lead_time_days
- buffers based on confidence & telemetry freshness

Algorithm:
1) urgency class:
   - emergency: rul_p10<=3 or critical & confidence>=threshold
   - high: rul_p10<=7
   - planned: rul_p50<=14
   - monitor: else
2) window calc:
   earliest_start = today + prep_days
   preferred = today + rul_p50 - buffer
   latest_finish = today + rul_p10 - buffer
3) greedy placement:
   sort tasks by urgency then earliest latest_finish
   place into earliest feasible day slots until capacity exhausted
4) output:
   scheduled tasks with start/end
   unassigned tasks with block_reason (capacity/availability/lead-time)
