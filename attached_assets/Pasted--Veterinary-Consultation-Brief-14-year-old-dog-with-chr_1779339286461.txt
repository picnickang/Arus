# Veterinary Consultation Brief

- 14-year-old dog with chronic wobbliness/unsteadiness present for several years
- Symptoms predate Gabapentin use
- Currently taking Gabapentin 100 mg daily and tolerating it well
- Condition appears chronic and relatively stable rather than acute

## Current Symptoms
- Wobbliness/unsteadiness
- Occasional stumbling/slipping
- Front legs sometimes slipping apart while standing
- Appears fatigued or uncomfortable while standing at times

## Not Observed
- Knuckling/proprioceptive deficits
- Falls/collapse episodes
- Severe hind limb weakness
- Seizure-like activity
- Nystagmus (eye flicking)
- Acute paralysis

History suggests possible chronic vestibular dysfunction and/or age-related mobility/joint issues.

The dog is very nervous/sensitive during handling but not aggressive. Owner would prefer a conservative, low-stress approach and would prefer to avoid general anesthesia unless clearly medically necessary.

## Requested Evaluation
Would appreciate:
- gait and mobility assessment
- basic neurological screening if tolerated
- assessment for arthritis, spinal discomfort, weakness, or mobility-related pain

## Requested Ear Examination
Would appreciate a thorough ear assessment for possible chronic vestibular or middle/inner ear disease, including:
- deep otoscopic examination (not just superficial external inspection)
- visualization of the ear canal and tympanic membrane/eardrum
- assessment for chronic inflammation, scarring, stenosis, debris, tympanic membrane abnormalities, or evidence of chronic otitis media/interna

If visualization is limited due to stress or discomfort, would appreciate discussion regarding:
- mild sedation/anxiolytics
- whether additional diagnostics would meaningfully change management given age and chronic stability

## Discussion Points
- Whether symptoms appear most consistent with chronic compensated peripheral vestibular disease
- Whether arthritis or musculoskeletal discomfort may be contributing to instability
- Whether Galliprant would be appropriate alongside Gabapentin
- Conservative quality-of-life focused management options
- Mobility/environmental modifications to reduce slipping and improve comfort
