## 1. High-level UX goals

Refactor the UI to look and behave like a modern marine PDM platform (Wärtsilä Expert Insight, ABB Ability Marine, SKF condition monitoring):

- Operator-facing screens should be simple, visual and equipment-focused.
- ML-facing screens should expose power but hide complexity behind “Advanced” toggles.
- Avoid form-heavy layouts that require pasting CSVs or manually tweaking ML hyperparameters by default.

Do NOT remove existing functionality; instead:
- reorganize it,
- make advanced controls collapsible,
- and add better grouping and visual hierarchy.
